What to Book

Choosing the Right Appointment

Not sure which appointment to book? Here's a quick guide.

15-Minute Appointment

£69 Adults  ·  £49 Children
Suitable for
  • Minor or straightforward issues
  • Coughs, colds, UTIs, earache
  • Simple skin complaints
  • Follow-up appointments
Not suitable for
  • Multiple concerns
  • New or complex symptoms
  • Women's or men's health
  • Chronic conditions

If in doubt, book 30 minutes.

Recommended

30-Minute Appointment

£119
Suitable for
  • New concerns and multiple issues
  • Ongoing or chronic conditions
  • Test results and referrals
  • Women's health and men's health
  • Sexual health
  • Anything you want to cover properly

This is the recommended appointment for most patients.

60-Minute Appointment

£199
Suitable for
  • Complex or multiple concerns
  • Chronic disease management
  • Full health review
  • Anything requiring extended discussion

Our most comprehensive appointment — unhurried, thorough care.

Telephone / Video Consultation

£49 — New & existing patients
Suitable for
  • Follow-up appointments
  • Results review
  • Prescription queries

If you're unsure which to book — call us. We'd rather help you choose the right appointment than have you feel rushed. 07424 172034

What to Expect

Your First Appointment

01

Book

Call, WhatsApp or email. We'll confirm your appointment slot and send you details of where to come. Fatima Health Centre, 228 Garstang Road, Fulwood, Preston, PR2 9QB.

02

Arrive

Come to Fatima Health Centre in Fulwood. There is ample parking on site. The clinic is also well served by local bus routes. Please arrive a few minutes early for your first visit.

03

Be Seen

You'll be seen by Dr Faizal Secretary personally. He will give you the full time you've booked. Prescription writing and referral letters included in your appointment fee if needed.
