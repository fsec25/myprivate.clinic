Common Questions

GP Consultations — FAQ

Do I need to de-register from my NHS GP?
No. We work entirely alongside the NHS. Keep your NHS GP — we're here for when you need to be seen sooner or need more time.
Can I get a same-day appointment?
Yes, subject to availability. Call before midday and we'll usually get you in the same day — particularly on Fridays when we run a full clinic from 9am to 8pm. Other days are by appointment.
Can I book a telephone or video appointment?
Yes. Telephone and video appointments are available for existing patients at £49 for 15 minutes. New patients are encouraged to attend in person for their first appointment.
What if I need to see a specialist?
The doctor will write a referral letter as part of your appointment — no extra charge. He has established relationships with private specialists across the North West.
Are prescriptions included?
The prescription writing is included — if the doctor decides you need medication, he writes the prescription as part of your appointment at no extra charge. You then take it to any UK pharmacy and pay only the dispensing cost there.
What conditions do you see?
All general medical conditions — acute illness, chronic condition reviews, infections, skin problems, joint pain, repeat prescriptions, second opinions and more.
