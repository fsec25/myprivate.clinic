Common Questions

Women's Health — FAQ

Can I see a female GP?
Dr Faizal Secretary is our lead GP. If you would prefer a female clinician, please let us know when booking and we will do our best to accommodate.
Do I need a referral to book a women's health appointment?
No. Book directly with us — no NHS letter required.
How long does a smear test appointment take?
A smear test typically takes around 15 minutes including preparation and discussion of results timing.
Can you prescribe HRT?
Yes. The doctor can assess, advise and prescribe HRT as part of a consultation.
What if I need to be referred to a gynaecologist?
The doctor will arrange this as part of your appointment.
Is my appointment completely confidential?
Yes. All consultations are completely confidential. Information is not shared with your NHS GP without your consent.
