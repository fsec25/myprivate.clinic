Panels Available

What We Offer

General Health Panel

A broad overview of your health — full blood count, liver function, kidney function, and more. A good starting point for anyone wanting a baseline.

Well Man Check

Tailored for men — includes PSA, testosterone, cardiovascular markers, and key organ function tests in one comprehensive panel.

Well Woman Check

Designed for women — covers hormones, thyroid, iron, and cardiovascular risk alongside a full blood count and metabolic profile.

Vitamin & Mineral Check

Vitamin D, B12, iron, folate and more — useful if you're experiencing fatigue, low mood, or want to check for common deficiencies.

Thyroid Panel

TSH, T3, T4 and thyroid antibodies — a full picture of thyroid function for anyone with symptoms of over- or underactive thyroid.

Diabetes & Blood Sugar

HbA1c and fasting glucose — to check for diabetes, pre-diabetes, or monitor an existing diagnosis. Fasting required.

Cholesterol & Cardiovascular Risk

Full lipid profile including HDL, LDL, triglycerides, and a calculated cardiovascular risk score. Fasting required.

Hormone Panel

Male or female hormones assessed in detail — useful for fertility concerns, menopause, andropause, or unexplained symptoms.

Cancer Markers

PSA, CA125, CEA and other tumour markers available where clinically appropriate. Discussed in consultation before testing.

Common Questions

Blood Tests — FAQ

Do I need to fast before a blood test?
For cholesterol and blood glucose tests, fasting for 8–10 hours beforehand is required. We'll let you know at the time of booking.
How quickly will I get my results?
Most standard panels return within 48–72 hours. The doctor will contact you directly to discuss.
Can I just book a blood test without a consultation?
We recommend a brief consultation first so the doctor can ensure you're getting the right tests for your situation.
Will results be explained to me?
Yes. The doctor contacts you directly to go through your results. You will never receive results without explanation.
Can I get blood tests for my child?
Yes. Blood tests are available for children where clinically appropriate. Please book a children's appointment.
Can blood tests be done at a home visit?
Basic blood draws can be arranged during a home visit. Please mention this when booking.
