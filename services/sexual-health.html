Services

What We Offer

Basic STI Screen

Covers the most common infections — chlamydia, gonorrhoea, syphilis and HIV. A sensible starting point for most people seeking a routine check.

Comprehensive STI Screen

A full panel covering a broader range of infections including herpes, hepatitis B and C, and trichomonas. Recommended after higher-risk exposure or for thorough peace of mind.

HIV Testing

Confidential HIV testing with results explained in person. The doctor will discuss PEP if you've had a recent high-risk exposure.

Treatment

Where an infection is identified, treatment is prescribed and explained in the same appointment. No need for a separate follow-up.

Hepatitis B Vaccination

Available as part of your sexual health appointment. The doctor will advise on whether vaccination is appropriate for you.

Common Questions

Sexual Health — FAQ

Will this be shared with my NHS GP?
No. Your results are completely confidential and will not be shared without your explicit consent.
How quickly will I get results?
Most results returned within 24–72 hours. The doctor will contact you directly.
Can I be treated at the same appointment?
If symptoms suggest an infection, the doctor can prescribe treatment at the same appointment without waiting for results.
Do I need to have symptoms to be tested?
No. Many STIs have no symptoms. Routine screening is a sensible step, particularly after a new partner or unprotected sex.
Is testing available for both men and women?
Yes.
Do I need to explain why I want a test when booking?
No. Simply book a sexual health appointment. You don't need to provide any explanation at the booking stage.
