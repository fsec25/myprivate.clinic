Documents

What We Issue

My Private Clinic issues same-day sick notes, fit notes and medical letters in Preston — covering employer letters, DVLA medicals, insurance reports and visa medicals.

Fit Notes (Sick Notes)

Issued same-day following a consultation. The doctor will assess and issue the appropriate fit note at the appointment.

Return-to-Work Letters

Letters confirming fitness to return to work following illness or injury.

Insurance Reports & Letters

Medical letters for insurance claims, income protection policies or critical illness cover.

School & Nursery Letters

Medical letters confirming a child's illness, condition or absence — issued at the children's health appointment.

DVLA Medicals

Medical reports required by the DVLA for licence applications or renewals following a medical condition.

Visa Medicals

Medical examinations and reports for UK visa applications and immigration purposes.

General Employer Letters

Letters confirming medical conditions, treatment or restrictions for employer HR purposes.

Common Questions

Medical Reports — FAQ

Can I get a sick note without being genuinely unwell?
The doctor can only issue a fit note where it is clinically appropriate following an assessment.
How quickly can I get a fit note?
Fit notes are issued on the same day as your consultation in most cases.
Do you charge separately for letters issued at a GP appointment?
Fit notes are included in your consultation fee. More complex medical reports are charged separately.
Can you issue a DVLA report for my condition?
Yes. Please call us to discuss your specific requirements before booking.
Can I get a visa medical at My Private Clinic?
Yes. Please call us to confirm your requirements — some visa categories have specific documentation requirements.
How long do medical reports take?
Most documents are issued same-day or within 24 hours of your appointment.
