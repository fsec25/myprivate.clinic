Services

What We Cover

Prostate Health & PSA Check

A PSA blood test with a proper explanation of results — not just a number. Ideal if you're over 50 or have a family history of prostate issues.

Testosterone & Male Hormones

Fatigue, low mood, reduced libido, loss of muscle — a full hormonal panel can identify imbalances and open up treatment options.

Erectile Dysfunction

Assessed and treated in a single appointment. No embarrassment, no judgement — just a clinical conversation and a plan.

Male Menopause (Andropause)

Testosterone decline is real and treatable. If you're noticing changes in energy, mood or drive, this is worth investigating.

Cardiovascular Screening

Blood pressure, cholesterol, blood sugar and more — a comprehensive check of your heart health and overall risk profile.

Hair Loss

Medical assessment of male pattern hair loss, with prescription treatment options discussed and initiated in the same visit.

General Men's Health MOT

A full review covering everything — bloods, BP, weight, lifestyle and anything else on your mind. A proper once-over, done properly.

Common Questions

Men's Health — FAQ

Do I need to explain my problem when booking?
No. Just book a slot. You can tell the doctor everything in the appointment.
Can I get a PSA test without seeing a GP first?
You can book a consultation and the doctor will arrange the PSA as part of your blood panel. Results are discussed directly with you.
Is erectile dysfunction something you can treat?
Yes. The doctor will assess and discuss treatment options including medication.
What if I need to see a urologist or specialist?
The doctor will arrange this as part of your appointment.
Is my appointment confidential?
Completely. Nothing is shared with your NHS GP without your consent.
How long will my appointment take?
All men's health consultations are 30 minutes as standard. This gives the doctor enough time to take a proper history and discuss everything you want to raise.
