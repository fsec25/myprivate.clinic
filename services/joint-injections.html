What to Expect

Your Appointment, Step by Step

01

Your Consultation — £119

A thorough appointment with Dr Faizal Secretary. He reviews the joint — examining movement, taking a full history, and discussing your symptoms. Your medical history is reviewed in full, including any previous injections, contraindications, and relevant past medical history. Everything needed to make the right clinical decision for you.

02

Reviewing Your Scans

Where available, Dr Faizal accesses your NHS records and reviews any existing imaging. If no recent scan exists and one is needed to guide treatment, this is arranged before proceeding — no shortcuts.

03

The Injection — from £150

If the consultation confirms an injection is appropriate, it can often be carried out in the same appointment. No second visit, no unnecessary delay. The total cost for a straightforward consultation and injection is from £270.
