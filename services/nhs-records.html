What Makes Us Different

Private Care That Knows Your History

Most private GPs operate completely separately from the NHS. No access to your records. No visibility of your past consultations, test results, or what medications have been tried before. Every appointment starts from scratch.

My Private Clinic is different. As an accredited GP practice, we have the ability to access your NHS record — the same records your NHS GP uses — with your explicit consent. That means Dr Faizal Secretary walks into your appointment already informed.

No gaps. No repeating yourself. No missed allergies. Just a consultation that can spend its time on what matters.

Common Questions

NHS Records — FAQ

Can every private GP access NHS records?
No. This is a capability most private clinics do not have. Access to NHS systems like GP Connect requires accreditation, technical integration, and regulatory approval. My Private Clinic holds the necessary accreditation as a registered GP practice.
Do I have to share my NHS records?
Absolutely not. It is entirely optional. Many patients find it helpful, but your appointment is just as thorough without it. Let us know your preference when booking.
What if my NHS record is incomplete or wrong?
NHS records aren't always up to date. If we notice something that looks outdated or inconsistent, we'll flag it with you during the appointment. If you'd like a correction made to your NHS record, we can advise you on how to request that.
Will my NHS GP be notified that you accessed my records?
Yes — all access is logged in the NHS audit trail. Your NHS GP will be able to see that access was made, by whom, and when. This is a feature of the system, not a concern: it ensures full accountability.
Can you add notes to my NHS record?
Yes, where appropriate and with your agreement. We can send a consultation summary to your NHS GP, which they can add to your record. This supports continuity of care between your private and NHS treatment.
Is there an extra charge for NHS record access?
No. It is included as part of your consultation at no additional cost. It is simply part of how we work.
