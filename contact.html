Fatima Health Centre, Fulwood

Fatima Health Centre is located on Garstang Road in Fulwood — a newly built facility with ample parking on site. We are well served by local bus routes and are easily accessible from the surrounding areas of Preston, Fulwood, Broughton, Longridge and the Ribble Valley.
