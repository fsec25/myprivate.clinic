## Key Patterns

**What drives reactions AND comments (his best posts have both):**
- A real story from practice or his building journey
- A named tension or uncomfortable truth
- Vulnerability without self-pity
- A genuine open question at the end

**What underperforms:**
- Short posts without a story or insight
- Pure humour without substance
- Posts that don't invite a response

**Comment-to-reaction ratio:**
The haemoglobin post has a 0.9 comment-to-reaction ratio (9 comments on 10 reactions) — extraordinary. The content is strong, it just hasn't reached enough people yet. This post format (clinical story → unexpected resolution → reflective question) should be repeated.

## Recommendations

### Double down on what's working
1. **More Pillar 1 — the ethics and access angle is his strongest.** Posts like "I'm an NHS GP opening a private clinic" are rare on LinkedIn. Most GPs don't say this out loud. He should write more of these.
2. **More clinical story posts** in the haemoglobin format — specific patient moment → unexpected detail → universal insight → question. The format works, it just needs more reach.
3. **Every post should end with a question.** His top 4 posts all end with one. His bottom 3 don't.

### Fix what's not working
4. **No more pure humour posts without substance.** The candid photo post got 5 reactions. The screen time post got 17 but only 2 comments. Personality works best when it's woven into a real story, not standalone.
5. **Health tech content needs more depth.** "Ran out of Claude Code" is weak. A post like: *"I've been using AI to help build my clinic's policies. Here's what it got right and what surprised me"* would be far stronger and fit Pillar 3 properly.

### Posting frequency
6. He's posting every 2–3 days. That's a good pace for now. Don't increase it until reach improves — frequency without distribution doesn't compound.

### Engagement strategy
7. He should be commenting on other people's posts in his target categories (health tech, private GP, longevity) — this is the fastest way to grow reach without paid promotion. Apify monitoring will surface posts to engage on.
