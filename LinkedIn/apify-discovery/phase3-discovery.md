### Search 1: `"concierge GP"` — Only 8 results
The phrase "concierge GP" is too niche — most UK practitioners say "concierge medicine" or "private GP." Only yielded 1 useful new find (Senthil Kumar Velusamy). Not worth repeating.

### Search 2: `"GP founder"` — Good
LinkedIn fuzzy-matches "GP" as "General Partner" in finance, so VC/PE noise crept in. Still surfaced real GP founders. **Dr. James Stanley** is the standout — occupational health angle is a direct match for Faizal's corporate medical ambition.

### Search 3: `"Preston" "Lancashire"` + CEO filter — Best corporate search yet
14 results, good quality. These are real Preston/Lancashire CEOs posting local content. Worth running again monthly.
