What's Always Included

No Hidden Extras

Every appointment at My Private Clinic is priced simply. The fee you see is the fee you pay — including things other clinics charge extra for.

Prescription Writing Included

If the doctor decides you need medication, the prescription is written as part of your appointment fee. Take it to any UK pharmacy and pay only the dispensing cost.

Referral Letters Included

If you need referring to a specialist, the doctor writes the letter during your appointment at no extra charge.

No Registration Fee

Just book and come in. No joining fee, no annual charge, no admin fee. You only ever pay for the appointment you attend.

No cancellation charge for changes made 24 hours or more in advance.
