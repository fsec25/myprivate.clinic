2. **Preston/Fulwood is uncontested** — nobody owns this geography
3. **Same-day framing** as primary headline promise
4. **Dr Faizal as founder-face** — personal brand beats corporate groups
5. **"Coming soon" services** (IV drips, joint injections, minor surgery) show growth trajectory
6. **Honest, transparent pricing** — publish everything upfront
