Patient Reviews

Be One of Our First

My Private Clinic opened its doors in April 2026. We are at the very beginning of our journey — and we want you to be part of it.

Your Voice Matters

Our Story Is Just Starting. Yours Could Be First.

We haven't been around long enough to have hundreds of reviews. We think that's worth being honest about.

What we do have is a genuine commitment to every single patient who walks through our door — to give them the time, the attention and the care they deserve. And we'd love for you to tell us, and others, how we did.

If you've visited My Private Clinic, your review matters more to us than you might think. Not just for the clinic — but for the next person in Preston who is looking for care they can trust, and hasn't found it yet. Be one of the first. Help us build something the community can rely on.

Transparency

You Said. We Heard.

We believe that the best clinics are built on honest feedback — not just five-star ratings, but real conversations about what we're getting right and what we can do better. This section is where we respond. Publicly. Honestly.

Every piece of feedback we receive — a kind word, a concern, a suggestion — is read by the doctor personally. Where we can improve, we will. Where we've got something right, we'll say so. And where a patient has raised something that has changed the way we work, we'll tell you about it here.

Because accountability isn't just good practice. It's the right thing to do.
